Balloon Analogue Risk Task (BART) Instructions

Welcome to the Balloon Analogue Risk Task. In this task, you will see a virtual balloon on the screen. Your goal is to earn as much money as possible by inflating the balloon. Here's how it works:

1. Pump the Balloon:

  • Click the "Pump" button to inflate the balloon. Each pump earns you a certain sum in a temporary bank.
  • The more you pump, the bigger the balloon gets, and the more money you can earn.

2. Cashing Out:

  • At any time, you can click the "Collect Money" button to stop pumping and secure the money you've earned for that balloon into a permanent bank.
  • Money in the permanent bank is safe and cannot be lost.

3. Balloon Bursts:

  • The balloon can burst at any time. If the balloon bursts, you lose all the money in the temporary bank for that balloon.
  • Each pump increases the risk of the balloon bursting.

4. Trials: 10

  • You will complete 10 trials, each with a new balloon. Your total earnings will be the sum of the money secured in the permanent bank from all trials.

Remember, the goal is to balance the risk of the balloon bursting with the potential rewards from pumping. Good luck, and try to earn as much as you can!
